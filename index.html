About Us

Designing Spaces,
Inspiring Lives

BK Interior Design is a full-service interior design studio based in Nana Varachha, Surat. We believe that great design goes beyond aesthetics — it transforms how you live, work and feel in a space. Our team combines thoughtful planning with creative execution to deliver interiors that are both beautiful and functional.

From contemporary minimalism to rich traditional styles, we tailor every project to your vision, timeline and budget.
